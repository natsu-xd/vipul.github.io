Powerland ATV
Project Type: Industry
Role: Digital Modeller
Responsibilities: Reverse Engineering from Scan Data, Class A Surfacing and Refinement, Developing Concepts and Prototyping, Introduction of 3D Printing in Poweland Production Workflow.

During final year of Bachelor's of Mechanical Engineering I was recommended to Powerland atv Pvt. Ltd. by Mr. Deelip Menezes, Managing director of 3d systems.

During my time here I worked on improving the design and implemented few changes for easier manufacturing and overall look of the bike.

I was assigned to various task of modeling and reverse engineering of side panels for making mould for vaccum forming by 3d printed moulds. After class A surface model from scan data. Mould were made with draft and various constraints for vacuum forming.

I was working closely with Mr. Deelip Menezes and smartline Pvt. Ltd. who would later print the moulds and do the vaccum forming of panels.
