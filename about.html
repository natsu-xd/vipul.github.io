Backstory

From growing up watching TV shows like Supercars Megafactories, How it's made, doing things in family's fabrication business as a kid and trying to build a custom chassis for mountain bikes at age of 12, I grew a curiosity of designing and making things, which later led me to world of Industrial Design. Since my Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, I have been working on designing and building 3D Printers and CNC Machines. I learnt lot of things while doing these projects.

As I had a keen interest in Transportation Design a learnt Alias for Class A Surfacing. As I liked Additive Manufacturing and Rapid Prototyping, I worked on improving the technology I have at hand and work on my own brand of 3D printers.

For Internship during Final year of my engineering i had an opportunity to work as Digital Modeller at Powerland Agro Tractor Pvt. Ltd. which was a wonderful opportunity to demonstrate my skills and spend my time doing something i really loved to do.
