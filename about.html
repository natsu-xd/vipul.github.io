Backstory

I developed a passion for creating and designing things while growing up watching TV shows like "Supercars Megafactories," "How It's Made," and "Top Gear." My early experiences in my family's fabrication business, where I tried my hand at building a custom chassis for mountain bikes when I was just 12, fueled my curiosity. This curiosity eventually led me to the world of Industrial Design.

After completing my Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, I started working on designing and building 3D printers and CNC machines. Through these projects, I learned a lot and honed my skills.

I also had a strong interest in Transportation Design, which led me to learn Alias for Class A Surfacing. Additionally, I enjoyed working with Additive Manufacturing and Rapid Prototyping and dedicated my efforts to enhancing the technology I had access to, even starting my own brand of 3D printers.

During my final year of engineering, I had the opportunity to intern as a Digital Modeler at Powerland Agro Tractor Pvt. Ltd. This experience allowed me to showcase my skills and do what I truly loved.

Currently, I continue to collaborate with Powerland as a freelance digital modeler. I work on reverse engineering projects and create molds for their ATVs. Additionally, I'm involved in developing their future concepts.
