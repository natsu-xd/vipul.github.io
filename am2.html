Project Type: Personal
Project Status: First Prototype Completed, Second Prototype in Progress.

Designed with users usability, repairability and reliability in mind. A fully enclosed design with working area of 250x250x270mm3

All the components are mounted on a machined aluminium base plate which is held by the side panels. Everything on this machine is mounted to this plate and based of that plate acting as a one unit.

Inertia between parts is reduced by having this design. As there is no compliant frame with parts mounted at different locations. Everything in this design is positioned and located based of the base plate.
